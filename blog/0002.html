Specifications

My PC consists of:
  • CPU: Ryzen 5 1500X (Set at 3.50GHz, goes up to 3.60GHz)
  • RAM: Crucial Ballistix Sport LT White (1x8GB, DDR4, SDRAM, 1200MHz)
  • Motherboard: MSI A320M PRO-VH PLUS
  • GPU: Asus R5 230 (Ryzen version) with 2GB RAM
  • Case: Kolink Luminosity
  • SSD: Kingston 120GB SSD
  • HDD: WD Blue 1TB
  • Monitor: AOC Style i2281FWH
  • Speakers: Logitech Z333 (with a subwoofer that makes my house shake)
  • Keyboard + Mouse: Cooler Master Devastator 3
  • USB WiFi Dongle (although I use Ethernet directly connected to the router mostly): TP-Link TL-WN725N
